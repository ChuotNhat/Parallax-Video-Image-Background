# why choose sinfor travel?
Within easy reach of Victoria, Port Renfrew is welcoming, affordable, and as wild as you like. Here, you live through your senses: Cedar. Sea spray. Wood smoke. You move with the seasons, currents, and tides
This is your gateway to ancient forests, epic hikes, and mighty surf — from eagles and Chinook salmon to Sitka spruce and giant Douglas fir. From here, you can trek, paddle, comb the beaches, or cast your line.

# PLAY IN TRUE NATURE
This is the place West Coasters come to feel West Coast, to be surround with the things that define us – things we carry deep inside. Wilderness runs in our veins. With our brand new Adventure Centre, you can book your West Coast excursions right on-site. Go whale watching, paddle the estuaries, tour the tall trees by foot, or hit the pavement on an e-bike. Wild Renfrew is yours to discover.
